3.2 Encoding of UA-FLP on FBS with bay list
The FBS representation consists of two main parts. A
rectangular plant is divided in one direction into several
parallel bays, each with varying widths or heights. Withineach bay, multiple facilities of the same width or height are
arranged. The general encoding for FBS typically contains
two components: the facility sequence which includes thefacility numbers, and the bay division which consists of 1 s
or 0 s indicating whether a facility is the last one in its
corresponding bay.
