6 Discussion
This paper presents a novel learning-based SA for solving
the challenging UA-FLP with FBS. The problem’s com-plexity necessitates the careful design of various neigh-
borhood search structures to handle the permutation of
facilities and bays separately. To ensure an effective searchprocess, a self-learning controller is employed to intelli-
gently select the most promising operator from a set of
operators. This controller enables the generation of optimalneighbor solutions based on the current iteration state. With
a more ﬂexible solution representation and enhanced localsearch, LSA achieves practical layouts, especially for
large-scale instances within an aggressive reduced runtime.
The algorithm’s success is attributed to its ability toadaptively learn from previous iterations and leverage this
knowledge to guide the search towards promising regions
of the solution space.
